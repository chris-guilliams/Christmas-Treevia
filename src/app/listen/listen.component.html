Christmas Treevia

Your browser supports speech recognition! To play a Christmas Treevia game using speech, follow these instructions:

  1. Click the "Listen" button below.
  2. If prompted, grant the app permission to use your device's microphone.
  3. Listen closely for a Christmas themed question to be asked, once the timer starts you will have 10 seconds to answer.
    • You can answer as many times as you wish within the 10 seconds
    • If you get the question right you will be notified and the next question will be asked
    • If 10 seconds has passed and no suitable answer has been given then the next question will be asked
  4. It will take a moment for your speech to be processed. The answer you have given will be displayed on the screen so you know if you're being heard right.

You may also "Stop" listening at any time. As well as reset the game.
