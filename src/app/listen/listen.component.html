Christmas Treevia Instructions

  • Press the red button button located on the present near the tree. Be warned, it might take a bit of effort to press it!
  • You will be asked 3 questions of increasing diffilculty, once the question is asked you will have 10 seconds to answer.
    • You can answer each question as many times as you wish within the 10 seconds
    • Your answer will be submitted for verification when you finish speaking so make sure to pause between multiple answers!
    • If you get the question right the tree will light up green!
    • If 10 seconds have passed and no suitable answer has been given then the game will end.
  • Were the questions too hard? Too easy? Challenge others by submitting your own questions using our ornaments designated with a question mark! Write your question and answer on the back of the ornament and hang it on the tree!
